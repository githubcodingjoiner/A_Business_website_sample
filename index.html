Enhancing Customer Experience with AI Chatbots

A global retailer implemented AI-powered chatbots to provide real-time assistance across multiple platforms, including their website, app, and social media. These intelligent bots not only answered customer queries instantly but also provided personalized product recommendations based on user preferences and browsing history.
